PERNUG Project


Short for PERsonalised NUtrItion through kitchen Gardens, the PERNUG project aims to provide personalised nutrition via an app that links plant nutrient composition with recipes and information of the nutritional needs of individuals/sub-groups (e.g. vegans at risk of Fe and B12 deficiency).
PARTICIPATE

The Experiment

Benefit from consuming plants with far superior nutritional quality all year round, grow nutritionally superior cultivars/exotic plants and biofortify plants with nutrients such as Fe and B12 so they make significant contributions to recommended dietary allowance (RDA).

The experiment will run over the period of 8 months (from May 2021), during which you will receive an indoor garden system (Grow Wall™), plants and nutrient supplies for free. Along the way, you will help us understand your requirements and shape the design of a recipe recommender system that can link plant nutrient compositions with your nutritional needs.

What you will receive

Through the duration of the project, you will receive:

  • An indoor garden system (Grow Wall™)
  • Plants and nutrient supplies

Completely free of charge

Grow Wall™

Grow Wall™ is an all-in-one hydroponics growing system featuring a space-saving, vertical design. It can grow a variety of vegetables and herbs on different levels. The automated control system manages the sunlight simulating LED lights and water circulation.
